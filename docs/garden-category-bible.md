### Dropped from old list:
- **Lillepotid** → merged into Mullad (istutusnõud are sold alongside soils)
- **Kastekannud** → expanded to **Kastmistarvikud** (hoses, sprinklers, watering systems — much bigger)

### Considered but rejected:
- **Kasvuhooned** — niche, expensive, low purchase frequency
- **Küttepuud** — only 1/4 chains carry it, not spring-specific
- **Aiamööbel** — expensive, less price-sensitive, people don't cross-shop €500 furniture sets
- **Taimed** — live plants vary too much per store, can't compare
- **Terrass** — only 2/4 chains, more construction than gardening
